# Cooking Checklist

Run this checklist before declaring cooked proof-usage entries complete.

## Material Coverage

- Each cooked entry lists one or more stable material IDs.
- Each material ID appears in `proof-usage/references/source-map.md`.
- Source differences are preserved when assumptions, objects, or rates differ.
- Legacy `techniques.md` and `strategies.md` cards are migrated, linked, or clearly marked as legacy.

## Taxonomy Quality

- The entry is searchable by problem shape.
- The entry names required assumptions.
- The entry identifies the desired proof move.
- The entry includes failure modes.
- The entry says how to adapt the move to a new proof.

## Public Safety

- No absolute local paths.
- No private account names.
- No raw transcripts, OCR dumps, or run logs.
- Public attribution is short and source-level.

## Final Index

- `references/index.md` routes by task shape, assumptions, tags, desired move, and material ID.
- `references/source-map.md` links source material IDs to cooked entries and public sources.
